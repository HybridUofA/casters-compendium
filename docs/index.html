A desktop toolkit for Caster Chronicles

Build, organize, share, and play your way.

Caster's Compendium combines a searchable card library with a flexible deck editor, Speedrobo-compatible decklists, and one-click local Tabletop Simulator exports. The first simulator is planned for v0.2.0.
