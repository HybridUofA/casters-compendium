Project roadmap

The deckbuilder is the foundation.

Near-term work focuses on refinement and community collaboration before the simulator becomes the central development effort.

v0.1.3 · Released

Deckbuilder refinement

Hover previews, drag-to-search removal, corrected card alignment, a new application icon, and a shared architecture for card data and game rules.

Future deckbuilder work

Art and personalization

Continued collaboration with Speedrobo on assets and prototypes, plus optional element and Caster backgrounds when artwork and permissions are ready.

Future deckbuilder work

Application update awareness

A background version check could notify users when a newer release is available and link directly to its changelog and downloads. Package-specific self-updating is not planned for the first version.

v0.2.0 · Simulator begins

A first playable foundation

The initial simulator will be intentionally rudimentary. It is a large experimental project with no promised delivery date.

Plans can change: Tester feedback, available art, game-data changes, and the complexity of simulator rules may shift priorities or timing.
