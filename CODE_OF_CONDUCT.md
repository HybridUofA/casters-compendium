# Community code of conduct

Caster's Compendium welcomes players, developers, artists, publishers, and
curious contributors. Participation should make the project safer and easier
for others to enjoy.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Discuss ideas and code without attacking the person behind them.
- Give useful context when reporting problems and accept good-faith questions.
- Respect privacy, publisher permissions, artist credit, and community rules.
- Stop an interaction when another participant clearly asks for space.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, deliberate
misgendering, doxxing, credential sharing, piracy, and sustained disruption are
not acceptable. Do not pressure artists or rights holders to grant permissions.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer or through the
community's private moderation channels. Reports will be handled as
confidentially as practical. The maintainer may edit or remove contributions,
restrict participation, or ban a participant when necessary to protect the
project and its community.

This policy applies in the repository, project Discord, and other spaces where
someone is representing Caster's Compendium.
